Best Urdu Books Of All Time By Mohd Zainul Aabideen

Top 3 Storytelling Books

Lalah Zaar

Tells story just like you are watching those moments in real life,urdu is little bit hard but a must read.

Sachchi Hikayat

Covers story of almost all prophets,famous sahaba,sufis and many islamic moral stories with lessons(given by author who is also a famous saint himself).

Nojawano Ki Hikayaat

Stories of hallow youth,order of storytelling is also good like stories starts from the quran then goes from hadeeth and so on..
